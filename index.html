Valentin Tszin

actor | dancer | event creator | researcher


Where should this force be taken from? The force that moves your body and transforms it into the other forms – the forms that are unfamiliar to you, but which give you the feeling of connection with the Other. Where should this force be taken from? The force that becomes this very Other. How can one create and recreate the Other and have a power to make it disappear.
