DAS GEGENTEIL
Drawing from various movement techniques our aim is to explore the potentiality of bodily transformation through performative action. The goal of the project is not for the participants to “become” something or someone, but rather to focus on and analyze the process of “becoming” and to learn the difference between the two.

We believe that states of being, both physical and mental, have a respective opposition (or “Das Gegenteil”.)

For us, dancing is at once what you are at any given moment, and your opposite. Using a number of schools of movement, such as Taekwondo, drama & physical theatre, throat singing and Butoh. We want to explore 101 ways of existence through intersections of these diverse techniques to derive the most unhuman and transformative movement potential of each of us.


LABORATORY
This is the 3rd Das Gegenteil laboratory. By the end of 2019 as the result of our performance practice we collected new ideas to approach the transformative states of mind, body and performance making strategies that we´re aiming to consolidate in the form of an upcoming laboratory.

WORKSHOPS

    2. Voice trainings
    3. Rhythmical exercises
    4. Dissociative exercises
    5. Biomechanics exercises
    6. Exercises with human and inhuman body forms and presences
    7. Group choreographies
    8. Solo choreographies
    9. Dramaturgical principles and compositions
    10. Public showings (Fridays)
    11. Feedbacks
