Demonissia

Performance consists of the rests of the woman who has ascended to the Way of knowledge through a detective line of exposure of the reason of defilement of her sex.
Opposite heroes will lead "plastic" discussion and will be found out who became the reason of total changes of the heroine.

Who or what is stronger in this situation? A plait or freedom of words? Fire or a stone on almost dead neck? All that and many other things in the uneasy formula of new performance Poema Theatre "DEMONISSIA"

This performance starts a new quality and value of the Person and his importance. All happens "without a curtain" only with a kerosene stove and the hands crossed
